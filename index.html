ARiA is built differently. Scientists and engineers work side-by-side — not in sequence — so discoveries reach the field as working systems, not reports left on a shelf.

ARiA Management

Dr. Jason E. Summers

Ph.D. · Chief Scientist & Managing Member
ASW Signal Processing Computational Acoustics Room Acoustics
LinkedIn Profile →

Dr. Summers established ARiA to use real research to meet real-world challenges by bringing together innovation and rigor cultivated in academia and at top DoD laboratories with the agility, field knowledge, and customer focus refined while directing development of key Navy systems in industry.

Recognized worldwide as a leading expert in signal processing for Anti-Submarine Warfare (ASW) and in theoretical and computational modeling of acoustic processes in air and underwater, Dr. Summers has been central to the development of multiple computational models now in active use by the US Navy.

As Technical Lead for the Advanced-Development Team of NAVSEA's Surface ASW Synthetic Trainer (SAST) Program, he oversaw development of the first fully physics-based system for shipboard and schoolhouse training on surface-ship sonar. He also provided R&D support for the award-winning Submarine Multi-Mission Team Trainer (SMMTT).

Working in room acoustics, he developed and validated improved mathematical and computational models for predicting sound fields in coupled-room concert halls — broadly cited in the literature and widely adopted by the worldwide consulting community. Leo L. Beranek nominated him for the MacArthur "Genius" Award in 2004.

Dr. Summers serves on dissertation committees at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute and Aalto University, Finland, and as associate editor of the Journal of the Acoustical Society of America.

About ARiA

Applied Research
in Acoustics

ARiA is a research-and-development firm with core capabilities in signal and information processing, machine learning, physics-based computational simulation, and software engineering.

We tackle real-world problems that cut across our core capabilities, where the tight cohesion of our teams of software engineers and research scientists enables ARiA to deliver fielded solutions at the leading edge of technology.

Structured specifically to address the well-known challenge many firms have in transitioning applied research to advanced development, ARiA brings together top-quality research scientists with system and software engineers in an environment where research and development mutually benefit from joint leveraging of in-house expertise.

By strategically placing offices in communities with superior quality of life but low cost of living and aggressively managing indirect costs, ARiA maintains DCAA-approved indirect rates that lead the sector in value. With robust cash-flow reserves, significant owner-equity backing, and no debt, ARiA is positioned for growth.
